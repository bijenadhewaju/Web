BANG YEDAM

Bang Yedam(born: May 7, 2002) is a member of korean boy group Treasure under YG Entertainment. He was born in Seoul to a beautiful family of musicians. He participated in a contest name K-poop star season 2 where he instamtly went viral for his voice despite his young age(12 at the time). Later, after being the runner up, only behind the winner who is now the labelmate AKMU the sibling duo. he joined the company shortly after the contest as a artist but later on went to become trainee and debuted after 7 years of vigorous training. He is known as 'Korean Justin Beiber', 'God-Yedam' and many other names by members, fellow idols and fans for his sweet voice which is out of this world.
